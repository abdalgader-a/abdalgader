About

Welcome to my website! I am a lecturer of mathematics and computer sciences at the Faculty of Mathematical Sciences and Informatics, University of Khartoum. Previously, I worked as an AI resident at Meta AI, where I was a member of the Modern Recommendation System team under supervision of Dr. Vassilis Plachouras. Prior to that, I collaborated as an AI research associate with LIVIA, École de technologie supérieure (ÉTS). Before joining LIVIA, I was a research intern at Mila-Quebec AI Institute under the supervision of Prof. Samira Ebrahimi Kahou.

My broad interest lies in the development of intelligent machine systems. Presently, my focus is directed towards exploring the theory and practical applications of self-supervised learning within the context of graphs and Graph Neural Networks (GNNs).
